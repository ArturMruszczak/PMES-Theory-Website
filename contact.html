Submission Guidelines

To ensure efficient communication, please follow these guidelines:

  • Subject line: Include "PMES Inquiry: [Brief Topic]"
  • Introduction: State your name, position, and institutional affiliation
  • Purpose: Clearly describe the nature of your inquiry
  • Reference: Mention specific PMES publications if applicable
  • Attachments: Limit file size to 5MB total

Note: Due to the volume of correspondence, we prioritize inquiries from academic institutions, research organizations, and established media outlets. While we appreciate general interest, we may not be able to respond to all personal inquiries about theoretical physics.
